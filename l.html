▶ word formation rule {

compound word or abbreviation is made by impotant word in expression that explain the new word. it must remain order. for example, music mean "sound that average person like". in veluil, it is expressed as "ae hnib mjib kd anga" but making word using the 5 word is inefficient. word for sound and word for like is important word so word for music is made by the two word.

hnmb !r=music.//c sound,like,

In compound words or abbreviations, if the order does not matter, the function comes last. For example, the word "fire" is a compound of "plasma" and "hot"; since "hot" is a function, the word "plasma" comes first.

if "and", "or", relative pronoun is in expression that explain new abbreviation that will be created then word whose bit is smaller is used earlier. for example, "we" mean "me and you". bit of word for me is smaller than word for you so word for you come after word for me.

bnca !r=we.//c and,i,you,AD,

if there are antonym of new word then the bit of new word must be smaller than the antonym. for example,

gc o,o,n!r=why x2 is x1.

jf o,o,n!r=result of x2 being x1.//a why,

gc in binary is 01100100. jf in binary is 10011010.

bit of jf that is converted into number is bigger.

}◀ word formation rule.
